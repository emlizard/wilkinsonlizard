2-Way Unequal Wilkinson Power Divider

Formulas Used

Let the unequal split ratio be \[ r \;=\;\frac{P_1}{P_2},\quad Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}} \;=\;\sqrt{Z_0 \,\cdot\, Z_0}\;=\;Z_0. \]

\[ Z_1 \;=\; Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}}\, \sqrt{\,r^{-1.5} + r^{-0.5}\,}, \] \[ Z_2 \;=\; Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}}\, \sqrt{1 + r}\;\,r^{0.25}, \] \[ Z_3 \;=\; Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}}\;r^{-0.25}, \quad Z_4 \;=\; Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}}\;r^{0.25}, \] \[ R_W \;=\; Z_{0,\mathrm{eff}}\, \bigl(\sqrt{r} + \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{r}}\bigr). \]
